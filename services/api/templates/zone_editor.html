How It Works
  1. Click "Draw Parking Area" and drag a rectangle over the parking spaces
  2. Enter the number of spaces and click "Generate Spaces in Area"
  3. Boxes are auto-created with names like A01, A02, A03...
  4. Drag each box to fine-tune its position
  5. Resize by dragging corners to match real parking lines
  6. Delete unwanted boxes (select + Delete key)
  7. Click Save All Zones when done
